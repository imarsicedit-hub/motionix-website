Differentiation

Why Motionix.

Not another editor. A content system built around your growth.

01

We research before we create

Most agencies start with templates. We start with data — studying top performers in your exact niche before writing a single word of script.

02

Scripts + editing in one system

The script determines 80% of performance. Most editors just cut what you give them. We write what performs, then edit it to keep people watching.

03

Every edit is intentional

No random transitions, no trend-chasing effects. Every cut, caption, and sound choice is there to hold attention and move the viewer toward action.

04

We measure and improve

We track retention curves and engagement data. What works gets replicated. What doesn't gets fixed. This is how content compounds over time.

FAQ

Questions answered.

How much does it cost?
Packages start at €400/month. What's included depends on your volume and goals — scripts, editing, and posting are all available. Send me a video and we'll figure out what makes sense for you.
Do I need a big audience to start?
No. Most clients start from scratch or with under 1K followers. The work is the same — build a system that grows. The earlier you start with the right structure, the faster the growth compounds.
How long until I see results?
Most clients see meaningful improvement in watch time and reach within the first 2–3 videos. Growth compounds over time — the first month sets the foundation, the second and third is where it accelerates.
What if I'm not satisfied?
We start with a free video breakdown before any commitment. If after that you want to work together, we'll agree on clear deliverables upfront. No long-term lock-in — if the results aren't there, we'll fix it.
