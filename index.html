How it works.

Three phases. Everything handled. You just show up and film.

01

We define your strategy

  • Deep onboarding — your offer, audience, goals
  • Strategy call to align on direction
  • Niche research — we study top creators in your space
  • Full month of scripts built specifically for you
02

You film, we edit

  • You record with your phone — we guide framing and delivery
  • We edit for retention — pacing, captions, pattern interrupts

03

We post and track

  • You focus on your business — content runs in the background
